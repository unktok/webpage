gg Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 9, 2026

gg is an Android app by Unktok that helps users take a 30-day break from gaming and track their progress.

Operator
Unktok Inc.
Contact: shiro.takagi@unktok.com
Information handled by gg
gg may handle selected games or apps, Android app usage statistics, reset progress, streaks, hours saved, money saved, challenge settings, and subscription entitlement state.
How gg uses information
gg uses this information to show gaming activity, calculate reset progress, display streaks and saved time, estimate money saved, and provide subscription access inside the app.
Local processing
gg stores selected games, usage-derived progress, challenge settings, and subscription access state locally on the user's device. Unktok does not upload app usage history, selected games, or progress data to Unktok servers.
Android Usage Access
gg asks the user to enable Android Usage Access permission so the app can calculate play time for selected games. The user can disable this permission at any time in Android settings.
Subscriptions and payments
gg uses Google Play Billing for monthly and annual subscriptions. Payment processing is handled by Google Play. Unktok does not receive or store full payment card details.
Data sharing
Unktok does not sell user data. gg does not share app usage history, selected games, or progress data with third parties.
Accounts
gg does not require users to create an account or sign in to an Unktok service.
Data retention
Information stored locally by gg remains on the user's device unless the user clears app data or uninstalls the app. Subscription records may also be retained by Google Play according to Google's policies.
User controls
Users can change selected games inside the app, disable Android Usage Access in Android settings, manage subscriptions through Google Play, or delete local app data from Android settings.
Changes
Unktok may update this policy when necessary. Material changes will be posted on this website.
