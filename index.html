About Unktok

Our current scientific system explores only a narrow slice of the vast landscape of possible scientific systems. There are countless ways science could be structured—different methodologies, incentives, collaboration models, and roles for technology—yet we remain confined to a small subset that emerged historically. This limited exploration risks leaving us blind to more efficient, equitable, or innovative systems. To unlock the full potential of scientific discovery, we need to step beyond the traditional paradigms and actively explore and build alternative structures.

Unktok is a meta-science company to tackle this challenge. Our mission is to build a self-evolving, autonomous scientific system that continually expands knowledge for the betterment of humanity. Toward this future, we are committed to accelerating the development of AI scientists, autonomous scientific systems incorporating these researchers, and the foundational infrastructure that enables them.
