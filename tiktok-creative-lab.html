TikTok Creative Lab

Creative testing for user-approved TikTok workflows.

Unktok Creative Lab helps creators and ecommerce operators prepare short-form creative tests, review AI-assisted hooks, scripts, captions, and media, and upload approved assets to their own TikTok account.

Public app page for TikTok for Developers review.

Open review demo
What it does
Users organize campaign ideas, generate and review short-form creative assets, select one video or photo, confirm the connected TikTok account, and explicitly approve upload to TikTok.
User control
The service does not post without user action. In the initial release, uploads are user-initiated and intended for the user to review and complete in TikTok.
TikTok data
The service uses TikTok Login Kit to show the connected account and may use the authenticated user's own public video metadata to help compare hooks, formats, captions, and calls to action.
Safety
Unktok Creative Lab does not collect TikTok passwords, does not scrape TikTok, does not access other users' private data, and does not use hidden or unofficial APIs.
